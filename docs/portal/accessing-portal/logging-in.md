# Logging In

1. Visit our login page at https://console.cdnetworks.com.

![null](</docs/resources/images/Login Page.png>)

1. Enter your username in the top field and your password in the bottom field. For security, each typed password character is masked with a dot (‚óè). To unmask the password, click the ![null](</docs/resources/images/eye icon.png>) icon.

**Note**: If you forgot your password, click [here](https://console.cdnetworks.com/cdn/forgotpassword).

3. Click the **Sign In** button.
